Welcome to QRStats!

You can use this app to keep track of the number of visitors to your website.

Login

If you've already created an ID, login to view your /stats.

How it works

You give us a URL, and we generate an ID. You can then share a URL that looks like https://qrstats.drewmca.dev/redirect/9aa7e609, and when they visit the site, we'll write that down and redirect them to your URL.

Then, you can visit https://qrstats.drewmca.dev/stats, and see how many people have visited your site.

Create

Fill out the form below to setup your website!

Password is optional, but if you don't provide one, anyone can view the stats for the generated URL!
